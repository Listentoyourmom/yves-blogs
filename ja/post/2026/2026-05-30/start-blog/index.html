Hey everyone, and welcome to my site! I’m kicking things off with this first post to give you a quick tour of what this blog is all about.

This is a space where I share all things China—from culture and daily life to tech, scenic spots, and whatever else I find interesting. You can easily filter topics using tags or just use the search bar to find exactly what you’re looking for. I really hope you’ll enjoy reading along!

Tags & Categories

Since this blog is all about sharing life, I’ve set up tags covering the essentials: clothing, food, housing, transportation, travel, entertainment, finance, weather, healthcare, and work. Of course, if I think of any other good categories down the road, I’ll be sure to add them!

Update Schedule

My goal is to post twice a week, with at least one post as a bare minimum. While, like most people in China, I have a pretty busy work life, but I’ll do my best to stick to this schedule.

Let’s Stay in Touch

If there’s something you’re curious about but can’t find on the blog, feel free to drop me an email. I’d absolutely love to hear from you, and I’ll do my best to answer your questions in future posts!
